Android​Camp

Friday, 1 April 2011, Bangalore

(A day before the Cricket World Cup finals)

AndroidCamp was a participant-supported unconference on the technology and business of Android. The agenda was made by participants on the spot at the event. As a participant, you got to decide what you wanted to hear or talk about. To see what discussions transpired, look at the discussion group and the session proposals.

Time: Wiki board for schedule went up at 9 AM. Sessions began from 10 AM. Session slots were for half hour each. The venue was available until 6 PM.

Venue: Althea Systems, 2nd Floor, #773 80ft Road, Koramangala 4th Block, Bangalore - 560 034. Down the road from Maharaja restaurant and opposite Umerkot restaurant. Map.
